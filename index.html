My name is Quentin Henry and I'm from New York City. I think I'm interested in memetics, or at the very least, things that repeat and change a little each time. I explore it through graphic design, photography, and writing. I've learned a broad variety of skills while working at various jobs in various industries all while I was studying various disciplines at various educational facilities in various counties. I was lucky enough to travel to various countries and encounter various experiences that have greatly shaped the way that I live and work. Through these experiences and skills, I've decided that I'm interested in graphic design, fast food chains, urban and suburban infrastructure, linguistics, ancient history, and maybe a few more things. Recently I've found the answers to some of my questions at the peaks of mountains, watching clouds that seem to form from nothing at all.
