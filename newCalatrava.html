Calatrava

Graphic Design, 2023

Overview

Calatrava: Impossible Architecture is an exhibition identity system that translates Santiago Calatrava's distinctive architectural language into graphic design. Through a restrained palette of blue and white, the system employs contrasting elements—delicate repeating lines against bold geometric forms—to mirror Calatrava's signature balance of structural lightness and monumental scale.

Typographic Principles

The typography reflects Calatrava's architectural philosophy:

  • Geometric clarity echoing structural forms
  • Modernist simplicity and rational order
  • Bold statements balanced with refined details
  • Emphasis on negative space and rhythm

Usage Guidelines

  • Use bold weight for architectural feature highlights
  • Maintain generous white space in layouts
  • Align type to structural grid systems
  • Create hierarchy through scale contrast
